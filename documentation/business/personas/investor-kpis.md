# Investor KPIs & Reporting Metrics

> Marketplace KPIs benchmarked for Vietnam bus-booking platform. Series A readiness framework. Research date: 2026-06-17.

---

## Growth Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) | Total ticket face value transacted through platform | Series A threshold ~$500K-2M/month |
| Booking count | Total completed bookings; track WoW and MoM growth | MoM growth target 15-25% |
| Active operators | Operators with at least 1 booking in trailing 30 days | Track as % of total onboarded operators (>60% healthy) |
| Route coverage | Unique origin-destination pairs with at least 1 active trip | Target top 50 corridors in Y1 |
| New customer acquisition | First-time bookers; split organic vs. paid channel | Track CAC by channel, payback period per cohort |
| Operator acquisition | New operators completing onboarding per month | Target 10-20/month pre-Series A, 30-50 post |
| GMV MoM growth | Month-over-month GMV percentage increase | >=15% sustained for 6+ months signals Series A readiness |

## Unit Economics

| Metric | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Take rate (net) | Platform net revenue / GMV after payment processing fees | Vietnam bus realistic range 8-12%; VeXeRe ~10% |
| CAC (Customer) | Cost to acquire one new booking customer | $2-8 via MoMo/Zalo promo campaigns; $5-15 via Meta/Google ads |
| CAC (Operator) | Cost to onboard one new operator | <$100 per SME operator; $500-2,000 per enterprise/large fleet |
| LTV (Customer) | Lifetime value: avg ticket VND 250K x 6x/year x 3yr retention x 30% gross margin | ~VND 1.35M (~$54) per customer |
| LTV/CAC ratio | Customer lifetime value divided by acquisition cost | Minimum 3:1; good 5:1+ |
| CAC payback period | Months to recover customer acquisition cost from gross margin | <12 months target; >24 months is a red flag |
| Gross margin | Net revenue minus direct costs (payment gateway fees 1.5-2.5%, SMS/OTP, infrastructure) | Target 40-60% after payment processing fees |

## Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Repeat booking rate | % of customers with 2+ bookings within 12 months | >40% by Month 12; strong >60% |
| Booking frequency (active) | Average bookings per active customer per year | Vietnam intercity natural frequency 4-8x; platform capture 2-4x |
| DAU/MAU ratio | Daily active users / monthly active users | Travel apps healthy range 15-25% (vs. social 50%+) |
| Conversion (search to booking) | % of search sessions resulting in completed booking | Target 8-15%; <5% indicates pricing or UX problem |
| Payment abandonment | % of initiated payments that fail to complete | <25% target; high rate signals trust friction or gateway issues |
| NPS (Net Promoter Score) | Customer willingness to recommend on -100 to +100 scale | Vietnam transport >30 good; >50 excellent (Grab benchmark ~45-55) |
| Operator satisfaction | Quarterly operator NPS/CSAT survey | Target NPS >35 |

## Operational Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Support ticket volume | Tickets per 1,000 completed bookings | <20 per 1,000 bookings |
| Avg resolution time | Mean time from ticket creation to resolution | <24hr standard issues; <4hr same-day trip emergencies |
| Refund rate | Refund VND value as % of total GMV | <2% of GMV |
| Overbooking rate | Bookings exceeding actual seat capacity as % of total | <0.1% (any overbooking is trust-destroying) |
| Dispute rate | Formal disputes filed as % of bookings | <0.5% (MTIP reporting threshold) |
| Payment failure rate | Failed payment attempts as % of total attempts | <3% with MoMo/VNPay gateways |
| On-time departure | % of trips departing within 15min of scheduled time | >90% (feeds operator scorecard and search ranking) |

## Financial Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target / Benchmark |
|---|---|---|
| Net revenue (ARR) | Annualized platform revenue (commission + fees) | Series A range $2-3M ARR with strong growth trajectory |
| Gross profit margin | (Net revenue - direct costs) / net revenue | Target 40-60% |
| Burn rate / runway | Monthly cash burn; months of runway at current rate | Maintain >18 months runway |
| Revenue MoM growth | Month-over-month net revenue percentage increase | >=15-20% sustained |
| GMV per operator | Average GMV generated per active operator | Tracks supply-side monetization density and operator health |
| Commission concentration | % of total commission from top 5 operators | <40% (high concentration = key-operator dependency risk) |
