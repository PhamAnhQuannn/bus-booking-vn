# Customer Personas & Segmentation

> Supplements [stakeholder map](../stakeholder-map.md). Research date: 2026-06-17.

## Market Context

MoMo leads Vietnamese digital wallets with 69% active wallet penetration; ZaloPay follows at 44%. Digital wallets account for 46.12% of online travel booking transactions in 2025, overtaking bank transfers and cards. Vietnam's intercity bus market serves ~1.5B passenger-trips/year, with online penetration still below 15% --- significant headroom for digital capture.

---

## Persona 1: Budget Domestic --- "Chi Lan" (Provincial Worker)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Demographics | 20-45yo, migrant/seasonal worker or student, HCMC/Hanoi to home province, income VND 5-12M/month |
| Booking frequency | 4-8x/year, heavy peaks at Tet, 30/4-1/5, and 2/9 holidays |
| Channel preference | MoMo in-app search, Facebook Marketplace groups, physical counter as fallback |
| Payment method | MoMo wallet (top-up from salary ATM), cash at counter, domestic ATM card |
| Price sensitivity | 5/5 --- compares 3+ options before booking, waits for promo codes |
| Trust factors | Review count, recognizable operator name, MoMo purchase protection badge |
| Key pain points | Tet overbooking and price gouging, last-minute cancellation with no refund, long station queues |
| Platform needs | Price comparison across operators, promo code support, MoMo cashback integration, full Vietnamese UI, clear refund assurance |

## Persona 2: Business --- "Anh Minh" (Regional Sales Manager)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Demographics | 28-50yo, urban professional, HCMC-Can Tho or Hanoi-Hai Phong for work, income VND 20-50M/month |
| Booking frequency | 2-4x/month, consistent year-round |
| Channel preference | Web browser or mobile app, advance booking 1-3 days out |
| Payment method | Corporate Visa/MC card, VNPay QR, requires VAT invoice (hoa don do) for reimbursement |
| Price sensitivity | 2/5 --- schedule reliability and comfort matter more than price |
| Trust factors | On-time departure record, VAT invoice availability, confirmed seat guarantee |
| Key pain points | No VAT invoice from most operators, schedule unreliability, no corporate billing or expense integration |
| Platform needs | E-invoice auto-generation (Decree 123/2020 compliant), schedule reliability indicator, seat class filter, downloadable receipt/invoice PDF |

## Persona 3: Young Urban Professional --- "Trang" (Millennial/Gen Z)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Demographics | 20-32yo, urban (HCMC/Hanoi/Da Nang), income VND 12-30M/month |
| Booking frequency | 1-3x/month (weekend getaways, Da Lat/Vung Tau/Ninh Binh) |
| Channel preference | Mobile app first, Zalo Mini App, TikTok discovery to in-app booking |
| Payment method | MoMo, ZaloPay, VNPay QR, Apple Pay |
| Price sensitivity | 3/5 --- willing to pay 20-30% premium for VIP/limousine comfort |
| Trust factors | Peer reviews with photos, smooth modern UX, real-time visual seat map |
| Key pain points | Clunky incumbent booking UI, no interactive seat picker, no amenity information, opaque refund process |
| Platform needs | Polished mobile-first UX, visual seat map with selection, amenity badges (WiFi/USB/blanket), one-click rebook, social proof (reviews + photos) |

## Persona 4: International Tourist --- "Marco" (Backpacker)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Demographics | 20-40yo, solo or pair, banana pancake trail (Hanoi-Hue-Hoi An-Da Lat-HCMC), budget-to-mid spending |
| Booking frequency | 6-12 bus legs per 2-4 week Vietnam trip |
| Channel preference | Hostel recommendation, Baolau/VeXeRe English UI, Google search, TripAdvisor/Hostelworld links |
| Payment method | Visa/MC credit card, Wise/Revolut debit card, no MoMo/ZaloPay (can't register without VN phone) |
| Price sensitivity | 3/5 --- budget-conscious but pays premium for safety and English support |
| Trust factors | English-language support, international card acceptance without surcharge, clear cancellation/refund policy in English, hostel/travel-blog endorsement |
| Key pain points | Vietnamese-only UI on most platforms, international card surcharges or rejection, no English hotline, obscure pickup locations not on Google Maps |
| Platform needs | Full English UI (human-translated, not machine), Stripe/international card gateway, GPS pickup pin with Google Maps deep link, WhatsApp or English email support channel |

## Persona 5: Elderly/First-Time Digital --- "Ba Hoa" (Pensioner)

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Demographics | 55-75yo, retired, visits children/grandchildren 2-4x/year, income VND 3-8M/month (pension + family support) |
| Booking frequency | 2-5x/year |
| Channel preference | Children/grandchildren book on their behalf, physical station counter strongly preferred |
| Payment method | Cash, occasionally bank transfer with family assistance |
| Price sensitivity | 4/5 --- fixed income but values safety, comfort, and familiar operators |
| Trust factors | Physical paper ticket in hand, familiar operator name seen at station, family member's endorsement |
| Key pain points | Confusing app UI with small text, fear of online scams/fraud, no paper receipt, difficulty navigating bus stations |
| Platform needs | Large-font accessibility mode (WCAG AA minimum), printable PDF ticket, SMS confirmation with operator name and phone, assisted booking flow for family proxy, operator contact number on confirmation |

## Persona 6: Student --- "Em Quan"

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|
| Demographics | 17-24yo, university student, travels home 2-4x/semester, data-plan conscious (prepaid mobile) |
| Booking frequency | 4-8x/year (semester breaks + Tet + national holidays) |
| Channel preference | Zalo primary, Facebook groups, classmate recommendations, books 1-3 days ahead |
| Payment method | MoMo (family top-up), domestic ATM card, cash at counter |
| Price sensitivity | 5/5 --- actively hunts discount codes, MoMo cashback offers, and group booking deals |
| Trust factors | Classmate recommendation ("my friend booked here"), clear refund policy, Zalo chat support responsiveness |
| Key pain points | High data costs for heavy apps, limited payment options, no student-specific discounts on platforms, confusing refund flows |
| Platform needs | Zalo Mini App integration (low data footprint), student discount code program, data-light/fast-loading UI, Zalo OA booking confirmation and updates |
