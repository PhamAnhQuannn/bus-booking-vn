# Pricing & Commission Comparison

> Commission rates are market estimates where not publicly disclosed. Confidence levels noted. Research date: 2026-06-17.

| Dimension | BB | VeXeRe | redBus | FUTA | Traveloka |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Commission rate | 6% default (configurable per operator via admin; floor 5%, ceiling 20%) | ~8-12% estimated (affiliate floor 7% confirms minimum) | 10-20% confirmed (multiple sources) | 0% (vertically integrated) | ~10-20% estimated (bus) |
| Settlement | T+1 | Not disclosed (likely T+7 to T+14) | Not disclosed (refunds 7 working days) | N/A | Not disclosed |
| Upfront / subscription | None | BMS subscription for enhanced tools | Free registration | N/A | Free partner registration |
| Additional revenue | SaaS operator tools | BMS/AMS subscriptions, flash sale placement, insurance | BOSS/SeatSeller software (~20-30% revenue), advertising | Direct sales, FUTA Express delivery, taxi | Hotels, flights, ancillaries |

### Key Findings

1. **BB 6% default is competitively below VeXeRe (~8-12%) and redBus (10-20%).** Admin can adjust per operator for promotional periods.
2. **T+1 settlement + published terms = trust advantage.** No competitor publishes their settlement terms. VeXeRe advertises "direct bank deposits" as a BMS selling point, implying the baseline experience involves delays.
3. **VeXeRe rate is estimated, not confirmed.** The 8-12% range is inferred from their affiliate floor (7%) and market positioning. redBus 10-20% is corroborated by 4+ independent sources.
4. **FUTA pays 0% commission** because it is vertically integrated -- it is not an OTA but an operator with its own booking platform. This is the structural ceiling on what any OTA can charge operators with scale.
