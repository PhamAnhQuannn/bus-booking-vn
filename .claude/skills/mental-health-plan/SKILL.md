# /mental-health-plan — Founder Brain Is The Asset. Wreck It And Co Dies. Protect It Like Servers.

## Why you'd care

Founders crash at 12–24 months in and a crash means the company stalls or worse — and most plans are written after the first warning sign, not before. The pre-set therapist + peer group + exercise floor is the boring infrastructure that prevents the bad outcome.

Mental health ≠ luxury. Mental health = production infrastructure for the most load-bearing person in the company. Founders break quietly. Build the monitoring + the runbook before the incident.

## Pre-flight
Run at founding OR before fundraise / launch / first hire — high-stress events compound prior cracks. Pairs with `/founder-journaling-cadence`, `/burnout-early-warning`, `/personal-runway-check`.

## Inputs
- History (prior depression / anxiety / burnout episodes).
- Current support structure (therapist, partner, peer group, family).
- Risk factors (genetic, current life events, financial stress).
- Schedule reality (can you actually carve 1 hr/wk for therapy?).

## Process
1. **Baseline the asset** — current state, history, risk factors.
2. **Set non-negotiable floors** — sleep / exercise / social / nutrition.
3. **Wire support tier 1** — therapist or coach, weekly or biweekly.
4. **Wire support tier 2** — peer group of other founders.
5. **Wire support tier 3** — partner / family / close friend.
6. **Define crisis playbook** — what triggers escalation, who to call.
7. **Schedule the review** — quarterly check + after any major stress event.

## Why this exists
- Founder is the most load-bearing person in the co; brain is the asset
- 30%+ of founders meet criteria for depression at some point in the journey (academic data, varies)
- The cheapest time to build the support system is *before* the crisis
- Investors increasingly screen for founder resilience — this is professionalism, not weakness
- Co-founders / team / family pay the cost when founder breaks silently

## Baseline (honest self-assessment)
- Prior episodes: <none / mild / moderate / severe — when, duration, what helped>
- Current state: <stable / under stress / red-flag>
- Risk factors:
  - Genetic / family history (depression, anxiety, bipolar, addiction)
  - Major life events past 12 mo (loss, divorce, move, new parent, illness)
  - Financial stress (personal runway < 6 mo)
  - Isolation (new city, recent breakup, remote-solo work)
  - Substance use (>14 drinks/wk, daily other)
- Protective factors:
  - Stable partnership / family
  - Long-standing friendships
  - Hobby / non-work identity
  - Faith / meaning practice
  - Prior resilience evidence

## Non-negotiable floors

| Domain | Floor | Trigger if breached |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| Sleep | 7 hr / night, 5+ nights/wk | < 6 hr 3+ nights → cut something this week |
| Exercise | 3 × 30 min/wk minimum | Missed 2 wks → call therapist or peer |
| Social (non-work) | 1 × 1-hr non-work conversation/wk | Missed 3 wks → schedule with friend immediately |
| Nutrition | 2 real meals/day (not just snacks) | Multi-day breakdown → fridge stock + meal kit |
| Sunlight | 20 min outdoors/day | < 3 days/wk → walking meetings or morning walk |
| Caffeine | <400 mg/day, cutoff 2pm | Past cutoff regularly → indicates sleep debt |
| Alcohol | <7 drinks/wk; never daily | Daily for 2+ wks → reach out for help |

**Rule:** floors are non-negotiable like a server's CPU floor. If a quarter is so intense you breach 3+ floors, that quarter is unsustainable — cut scope.

## Support tier 1 — Therapist or coach
**Therapist (clinical):** trained in depression / anxiety / trauma. Use if any prior clinical episode, family history, or current diagnosis.
**Coach (performance):** trained in founder/executive performance. Use for goal/identity/strategy work.

Many founders need **both** — different skill sets.

**Cadence:** weekly or biweekly. Not "as needed" — recurring slot.
**Modality:** in-person preferred but virtual is fine.
**Cost:** $150-300/session typical (US). Budget line item, not optional.
**Therapist:** <name + contact>
**Coach:** <name + contact>
**First-session-no-later-than:** <YYYY-MM-DD>

If no therapist yet: 5 names from referral + interview 3 + commit. Compatibility > credentials.

## Support tier 2 — Founder peer group
- 4-8 other founders, similar stage, different domains
- Meet 1-2 hr biweekly or monthly
- Confidential, no investors, no employees
- Structured format (round-robin updates + 1 deep-dive per session)

**Options:**
- YC Founder Circles / Bookface
- Stripe Founders Network / On Deck
- Reforge / Hampton / Sundial
- Self-organized (best signal, hardest setup)
- 1:1 founder peer (if group not workable)

**My group:** <name + members + cadence>
**Or self-org plan:** <names to recruit by date>

## Support tier 3 — Personal anchors
- Spouse / partner: <name, knows the journey>
- Family closest: <name>
- 1-2 close friends outside the co: <names>

**Rule:** at least 1 person outside the co who knows when you're struggling. If only employees and investors know your state, you have no support — you have stakeholders.

## Daily / weekly practices

**Daily:**
- 5-10 min morning intent (see `/founder-journaling-cadence`)
- Move body once (walk / gym / yoga / sport)
- Real lunch break (15 min off-screen)
- Hard stop at <hour>, no exception 3+ nights/wk
- Phone out of bedroom

**Weekly:**
- Therapy / coach session
- 1 × 1-hr non-work conversation
- 1 weekend day with zero work (if possible)
- Weekly retro entry (see `/founder-journaling-cadence`)

**Monthly:**
- Peer group session
- 1 full day off-grid (no Slack, no email)
- Monthly journal pattern pass

**Quarterly:**
- Long weekend / mini-break
- Quarterly mental health review (this doc)

**Annually:**
- 1+ week real vacation (laptop off, see `/vacation-policy-pre`)
- Annual physical
- Bloodwork (Vit D, thyroid, testosterone if applicable — physical signal often masquerades as mental)

## Crisis playbook
**Yellow signals (act this week):**
- Sleep < 6 hr 4+ nights in a row
- Crying / panic / rage out of proportion
- Avoiding all team contact
- Drinking daily
- Suicidal ideation (passive — "what if I just stopped")
- "I can't do this" thought daily

**Action:** call therapist within 48 hr. Cut 1 commitment this week. Tell 1 person in tier 3.

**Red signals (act today):**
- Active suicidal ideation (plan, means, intent)
- Self-harm
- Sustained panic / disassociation
- Inability to leave bed for 3+ days
- Substance use loss-of-control

**Action:**
1. Call therapist same day
2. Call partner / closest tier-3 person now
3. If unsafe: **call 988 (US Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or local equivalent**
4. Cancel the week. Co survives a week. You may not.

**Local crisis numbers:**
- US: 988
- UK: 116 123 (Samaritans)
- EU general: 116 123
- AU: 13 11 14 (Lifeline)
- <your country>: <number>

**Emergency contact for co (if you're incapacitated):**
- Co-founder / senior teammate: <name>
- Pre-written letter: <where stored — e.g. encrypted note, attorney>

## Substance & medication notes
- Prescribed meds: <list, who manages>
- OTC use: <list>
- Substances of concern: <honest list>

**Rule:** anything that requires daily use to function should be reviewed with a doctor + therapist quarterly. No shame, just monitoring.

## Money & mental health
Personal financial stress is a top driver of founder mental health crises.
- Personal runway: <months>
- Floor below which mental health degrades: <months>
- If breached → trigger `/personal-runway-check` + consider raising / paying self / cutting burn

## Co-founder / team norms (if applicable)
- "I'm having a hard week" is sayable — model it
- Mental health days don't count against PTO
- Team has at least 1 leader other than you who can step in for 1 week
- Bus factor for crisis = 0; this is fixable

## Anti-patterns
- ❌ "I'll start therapy when things calm down" — calm never arrives
- ❌ Founder hides struggle from co-founder → trust collapses later
- ❌ Cofounder-only "therapy" — they have skin in the game, that's not therapy
- ❌ Daily alcohol as decompression
- ❌ Workout as identity (>2 hr/day) — exercise can become avoidance
- ❌ Productivity stack to mask depression (more discipline ≠ more well)
- ❌ Public performance of wellness while privately collapsing
- ❌ Skipping bloodwork / physical "no time" — many "mental" symptoms are physical

## Quarterly review
End of each quarter:
- All floors held? Which breached?
- Therapist + peer + tier-3 still active?
- Yellow signals appeared? How handled?
- Anything to update in this doc?
- Next quarter: cut something or sustainable?
